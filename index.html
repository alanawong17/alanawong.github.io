PORTFOLIO

Pekopeko Magazine

Pekopeko is an editorial food magazine blending curated recipes, photography, and storytelling inspired by beloved animated shows, movies, and games. It’s a vibrant intersection of pop culture and culinary arts, offering nostalgic and trend-forward recipes for fans and foodies alike. Whether you're a die-hard anime lover or just looking for fresh kitchen inspo, Pekopeko brings the joy of cooking to life through iconic stories.

Alpine Edge

Alpine Edge is a bold brand identity for a ski and snowboard company that captures the thrill of winter sports through edgy, dynamic design. Built around a spirit of adventure and innovation, the system features a modern logo, striking color palette, and clean typography crafted to stand out in a competitive landscape. The result is a brand that resonates with both seasoned athletes and newcomers, building a strong, stylish presence on and off the slopes.

The Never Ending Story

This exhibition typography project celebrates the timeless elegance of Dolle Etoile through a refined suite of design elements including posters, packaging, stationery, and environmental graphics. Each piece is thoughtfully crafted to highlight the intricate beauty of the art form, blending expressive typography with striking visuals. The result is a cohesive, immersive experience that invites audiences to engage with Dolle Etoile’s sophistication and allure.

Spotify Redesign

This UI/UX redesign of Spotify’s 2023 desktop app enhances usability while staying true to the brand’s vibrant identity. Focused on clarity, accessibility, and seamless navigation, the updated interface responds to user feedback and streamlines the listening experience. The result is a more intuitive platform that strengthens engagement and reinforces Spotify’s standing in the competitive music streaming space.

Pinterest Annual Report

This project features a complete redesign of Pinterest’s 2025 Annual Report, transforming financial data, statistics, and graphs into a cohesive, branded document. The design reflects Pinterest’s identity with clarity and creativity, making complex information engaging and easy to navigate.

Chopsticks + Forks Mid-Autumn Food Tour

This event design project for Chopsticks + Forks creates a cohesive brand system for a mid-autumn themed food tour. Inspired by the festival’s vibrant traditions, the design features playful visuals, bold typography, and a rich color palette. The branding celebrates cultural heritage and seasonal cuisine, inviting participants to connect through food, story, and community.
